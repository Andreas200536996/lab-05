The Best Greek To Ever Live

The story of Andreas

In the year 2003, the world was forever changed as in the small town of Parry Sound, I was born! Even as a newborn infant I was vastly superior to the other babies. From my ability to crawl like a race car to my elegantly groomed appearance, I was truly a sight to behold.

I began making headway with my ambition when I hit highschool and started to find my path forward, which so happened to be when an accident changed my future. Where I was supposed to be in a Grade 9 business class I was accidentally put into a Grade 10 programming class where I learned to love the proffession, and have now chosen to pursue it in college.

My fondest accomplishment is that I managed to achieve my Grade 9 in piano while maintaining a full courseload throughout highschool. While I am happy with my current accomplishment, I strive to obtain my diploma in software development and to further my knowledge by obtaining a degree in business.
